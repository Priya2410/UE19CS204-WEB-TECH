The longer he lived, the more bile he was becoming and the more poisonous
were his words. People avoided him, because his misfortune became
contagious. It was even unnatural and insulting to be happy next to him.

But one day, when he turned eighty years old, an incredible thing happened. Instantly everyone started hearing the rumour:

"An Old Man is happy today, he doesn’t
complain about anything, smiles, and even his face is freshened up.”

The whole village gathered together. The old man was asked:
Villager: What happened to you?

“Nothing special. Eighty years I’ve been chasing happiness, and it was useless.
And then I decided to live without happiness and just enjoy life. That’s why I’m happy now.” – An Old Man
